Extends CachedContent to include data about a user’s submissions and points in the course’s exercises.

Note that the data returned by this is dependent on the show_unrevealed parameter. When show_unrevealed is False, reveal rules are respected and exercise results are hidden when the reveal rule does not evaluate to true. When show_unrevealed is True, reveal rules are ignored and the results are always revealed.
